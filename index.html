Last Updated: December 2, 2025

At CosplayAI, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data. We have designed our service to collect the absolute minimum amount of information required to function.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect one piece of Personal Identifiable Information (PII):

  • Email Address: We collect your email address for the sole purpose of creating your account, maintaining your credit balance/subscription status, and providing customer support.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

2. Image Processing & Privacy

Your privacy regarding your photos is our top priority. Our image handling process is as follows:

  • No Storage: We do not store, save, or archive any photos you upload or the images generated by the app.
  • Zero Face Data Collection: We do not extract, store, or share facial geometry or biometric data.
  • Transient Processing: When you use the app, your image is sent directly to the Google Gemini API for immediate processing and returned to your device. Once the transaction is complete, the data is not retained by us.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide our services, we utilize the following third-party providers:

  • Google Gemini API: Used exclusively for the AI image generation process.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication (logging in with your email) and database management (storing your credit balance).

4. Data Security

Although we do not store your images, we ensure that your email address and credit balance are stored securely using industry-standard encryption provided by Google Firebase.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and your email address from our database at any time by contacting support.
