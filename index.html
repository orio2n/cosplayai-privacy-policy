Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

At CosplayAI, we take your privacy seriously. This policy details how we collect, use, and process your data, specifically regarding your photos and face data, in compliance with Google Play requirements.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following types of data to provide our services:

  • Uploaded Images: Photos you select from your gallery or camera.
  • Face Data: Mathematical representations of facial features extracted from your photos.
  • Technical Data: Device model, OS version, and crash logs (via Firebase).
  • Account Data: User ID and purchase history (via Google Play & Firebase).

2. How We Use Face Data

The core functionality of CosplayAI is to transform your appearance using Artificial Intelligence. We use your face data solely to:

  • Map your facial features onto a target cosplay style.
  • Adjust expressions (e.g., smile, wink).
  • Blend your likeness with generated backgrounds or outfits.

Important: We do NOT use your photos or face data to train public AI models. We do NOT sell your face data to third parties.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Processing: Images are uploaded securely to our cloud server for processing. They are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of generation.
  • Storage: The final generated images are saved locally on your device. If you use cloud sync features, they are stored privately in your Firebase account until you delete them.

4. Third-Party Services

We partner with trusted providers to ensure app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: For secure authentication and database management.
  • Google Play Services: For managing in-app subscriptions and credits.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of all your data associated with CosplayAI. You can perform this action within the app settings or by contacting support.
