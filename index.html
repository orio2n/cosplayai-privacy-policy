Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

At CosplayAI, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage your information. We have designed our system to be as private as possible while still offering features like your personal Gallery history.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: When you sign up via Google or Email, we collect your email address and a unique user identifier (UID) to secure your account and manage your credit balance.
  • User Content (Images): We store the images you generate for your My Creations collections. These images are not used for any other purpose.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data regarding feature usage (e.g., which styles are most popular) to improve the app experience.

2. How We Store Your Images

Unlike many other apps, we define strict boundaries on how your images are handled:

  • Personal Gallery Storage: Your uploaded images and generated results are stored securely in a private cloud container (Google Firebase Storage) linked specifically to your User UID. This allows us to display your "My Creations" history and lets you edit previous works.
  • No Public Sharing: We do not share your images publicly. They are only visible to you unless you explicitly choose to share them yourself.
  • AI Processing: To generate results, your image is temporarily processed by our AI providers (Google Gemini API). These providers are contractually obligated not to use your images for training their public models without consent.

3. Third-Party Processors

We use trusted third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database management, and secure image storage.
  • Stripe: For processing secure credit card payments. We do not store your financial information; it is handled directly by Stripe.
  • Pexels API: Used for the "Discover" search feature. Search queries may be sent to Pexels to retrieve reference images.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We believe in your right to be forgotten:

  • Manual Deletion: You can delete any individual image from your history at any time using the "Delete" (Trash Can) button in the Gallery or Studio. This action permanently removes the file from our servers immediately.
  • Account Deletion: You may request full account deletion by contacting support. This will wipe your credits, history, and all stored images.
